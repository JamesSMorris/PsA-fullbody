CUSTOM TRAINING PLAN

3-DAY FULL
BODY PROGRAM

Designed around Psoriatic Arthritis with a focus on upper body hypertrophy — chest, arms & shoulders.

3x Per Week Upper Body Focus Joint-Friendly
⚠ Medical Disclaimer: This program is designed with Psoriatic Arthritis in mind but is not a substitute for medical advice. Always consult your rheumatologist or physical therapist before starting a new program, especially during flare-ups. Stop any exercise that causes joint pain (not muscle fatigue).
PA-Aware Training Principles
Seated or supported exercises to protect knees & feet
Avoid heavy axial loading (barbell squats, deadlifts)
Never train through joint pain — only muscle burn
Control tempo: 2 sec up, 2 sec down — no jerking
Machines preferred over free weights for stability
Skip session on flare-up days; rest is training too
Warm up joints for 10 min before every session
Stay hydrated — inflammation responds to dehydration
Warmup & Cooldown (Every Session)
Warmup — 8–10 min
  • 5 min recumbent bike or arm ergometer (low impact)
  • Shoulder circles — 15 reps each direction
  • Arm cross-body stretch — 20 sec each
  • Wrist rotations — 15 reps
  • Seated torso rotations — 10 reps
  • Light band pull-aparts — 2×15
Cooldown — 5–7 min
  • Chest doorframe stretch — 30 sec each side
  • Overhead tricep stretch — 30 sec each
  • Seated pigeon pose (hip opener) — 30 sec
  • Seated calf raises & ankle circles
  • Deep breathing — 1 min
A

CHEST & TRICEPS + LOWER

MON / FIRST SESSION OF WEEK

Chest Press Machine CHEST
4 sets × 10–12 reps | 60 sec rest
Seated, back supported. Start light — prioritize full ROM.
Incline Dumbbell Press CHEST
3 sets × 10–12 reps | 60 sec rest
Use a bench at 30–45°. Control the descent slowly.
Cable Chest Fly (low to high) CHEST
3 sets × 12–15 reps | 45 sec rest
Standing or seated. Great stretch at the bottom.
Tricep Pushdown (rope) TRICEPS
3 sets × 12–15 reps | 45 sec rest
Cable machine. Squeeze at the bottom, elbows tucked.
Seated Leg Press (Light) JOINT SAFE
3 sets × 12–15 reps | 60 sec rest
Feet high on platform. Keep range of motion comfortable — never force deep knee bend.
Seated Calf Raise JOINT SAFE
3 sets × 15–20 reps | 45 sec rest
Slow and controlled. Helps maintain ankle mobility.
B

SHOULDERS & ARMS + CORE

WED / MID-WEEK SESSION

Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press SHOULDERS
4 sets × 10–12 reps | 60 sec rest
Back supported. Don't lock out elbows at top.
Lateral Raise (Cable or DB) SHOULDERS
3 sets × 12–15 reps | 45 sec rest
Light weight, slight bend in elbow. Cable gives better joint-friendly tension.
Face Pulls (Cable) REAR DELT
3 sets × 15 reps | 45 sec rest
Critical for shoulder health with PA. Use a rope at eye level.
Dumbbell Bicep Curl BICEPS
3 sets × 10–12 reps | 45 sec rest
Seated preferred. Supinate at the top. Avoid momentum.
Overhead Tricep Extension (cable) TRICEPS
3 sets × 12 reps | 45 sec rest
Long head stretch — great for arm size. Seated or standing.
Seated Ab Crunch Machine JOINT SAFE
3 sets × 15–20 reps | 45 sec rest
Or planks on forearms — zero knee/foot pressure.
C

BACK & BICEPS + LOWER

FRI / END OF WEEK

Lat Pulldown (wide grip) BACK
4 sets × 10–12 reps | 60 sec rest
Seated and supported. Pull to upper chest, lean back slightly.
Seated Cable Row BACK
3 sets × 10–12 reps | 60 sec rest
Keep back neutral. Great for posture and shoulder health.
Machine Row BACK
3 sets × 12 reps | 45 sec rest
Chest-supported version removes spine stress entirely.
Hammer Curl (DB) BICEPS
3 sets × 12 reps | 45 sec rest
Neutral grip — easier on wrist joints than standard curls.
Recumbent Bike JOINT SAFE
10–15 min | moderate pace
Low-impact cardio to maintain leg health without stressing knees or feet.
Hip Abductor Machine JOINT SAFE
3 sets × 15 reps | 45 sec rest
Seated. Strengthens hips — helps offload stress from knees over time.
🔥 During Flare-Up Days Skip the scheduled session entirely — inflammation is active and training will worsen it. If you want to move, consider gentle seated stretching, light band work (zero joint load), or just breathing exercises. Return to the program when pain subsides. Missing sessions is not failure — it's smart management.
